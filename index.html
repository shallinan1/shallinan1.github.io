Skyler Hallinan

    •
    I'm a Master's student (BS/MS) at the University of Washington studying computer science. I am fortunate to work with Yejin Choi. I have also previously worked with Hanna Hajishirzi. I do research in natural language processing, specifically towards enhancing and leveraging the capabilities of language models for meaningful problems, including commonsense reasoning, question answering, rewriting, debiasing, and summarization. Feel free to reach out to chat!
